History

the company was found by Steve Jobs in 1976, the same year he found the Apple company. During the company's early day, majority of company's product was diet pet food since people worry about their pet getting overweight. After 20 years of constantly growth, the company had experienced an economy downturn. Today, Durham House of Pets is the largest pet store of the nation. It provides more than 90% pet food consumption daily.
